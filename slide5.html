First microprocessor - 2,300 transistors, 4-bit
1972

First 8-bit processor - 3,500 transistors
1974
Intel 8080
Powered the Altair 8800 - 6,000 transistors
1978
Intel 8086
First 16-bit x86 processor - 29,000 transistors
