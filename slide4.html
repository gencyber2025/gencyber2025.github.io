🏆 The Parallel Inventors

👨‍🔬

Jack Kilby

Created the first working integrated circuit at Texas Instruments

September 12, 1958

🧑‍💼

Robert Noyce

Invented the silicon integrated circuit at Fairchild Semiconductor

January 23, 1959

Both invented the IC independently! Kilby won the Nobel Prize, Noyce co-founded Intel.

💡 Fun Fact: The "Tyranny of Numbers"

Before ICs, connecting thousands of components by hand was nearly impossible. The IC solved this "tyranny of numbers" by putting everything on one chip!
